100 Kotowaza, 100 Balances

To combine love of Japanese & acrobatics.

Kotowaza are Japanese proverbs.
For each kotowaza, I will design an acro-balance sequence to accompany it

Why did I make this website?

1) Study Japanese
2) Have proverbs engrained in my mind to guide me and help me interpret the world
3) Maintain balance, flexibility, breathing, strength, focus (proprioception)
4) Fun fun fun
5) To challenge myself and other people
6) Learn how to build a website
