100 Kotowaza, 100 Balances

To combine my love of Japanese & acrobatics.

Kotowaza are Japanese proverbs.

For every kotowaza I discuss, I will follow it with an acro-balance pose or flow

Why?

  1. To study Japanese
  2. Have proverbs engrained in my mind to guide me
  3. Maintain balance, flexibility, breathing, strength, focus
  4. Fun fun fun
  5. To challenge myself and other people
  6. Learn how to build a website

This website is for people who want to....

  • Challenge themselves in acrobatics/balancing
  • Discuss proverbs
  • Practice some Japanese
